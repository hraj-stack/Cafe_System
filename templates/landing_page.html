HRD Philosophy
"We believe that slowness is a form of respect — for the ingredient, for the eater, for the earth that grew it."
— HRD, Founder
♀
Woman-Led

Every role in our production — from cellar to label — is filled by women who chose craft over convenience.

🌍
Climate Positive

Our glass is 100% recycled. Our labels are seed paper. Our boxes are wild-seeded with Nordic meadow grass.

Living Culture

Our SCOBY cultures have been alive since 2017. We don't pasteurize. We don't filter. We keep things alive.
